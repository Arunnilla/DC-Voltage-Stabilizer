Introduction

Welcome to our project on DC Voltage Stabilizer. In modern electronic systems, maintaining a constant voltage is critical for the reliable operation of circuits. Voltage fluctuations can lead to malfunction, damage, or degraded performance of sensitive electronic components. Hence, stabilizers play an essential role in power management.
In this project, we have designed and developed a DC Voltage Stabilizer capable of delivering steady voltage output despite input variations. The project is carried out through both hardware implementation and software simulation.
Hardware Implementation:
We achieved voltage regulation using buck converter with ESP32 for PWM and the LM317 adjustable voltage regulator, a widely used and reliable IC. Practical experiments were conducted to stabilize different voltage levels suitable for various loads.


Simulation Work:
To explore and understand alternative methods of voltage regulation, we also performed simulations using the 555 Timer IC and the 723 Voltage Regulator IC. These simulations provided insights into different regulator circuit designs and their performance under various conditions.
